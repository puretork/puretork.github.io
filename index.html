Core Product

The PRPA (Puretork Rack & Pinion Actuator) is our flagship quarter-turn pneumatic actuator, featuring a proprietary, patent-pending integrated air reservoir design. Building on an innovation first introduced by our group in 2011, the PRPA has been refined for greater strength, longevity, and consistent performance, representing the next generation of pneumatic actuation.
