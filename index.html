Innovative Pneumatic Actuation Solutions for Global Industries

As a brand officially launched in 2025, Puretork combines decades of valve manufacturing expertise with pioneering actuator technology to deliver safe, efficient, and dependable motion control for industries worldwide.

Core Product

The PRPA (Puretork Rack & Pinion Actuator) is our flagship quarter-turn pneumatic actuator, featuring a proprietary integrated air reservoir design. Building on an innovation first introduced by our group in 2011, the PRPA has been refined for greater strength, longevity, and consistent performance, representing the next generation of pneumatic actuation.
